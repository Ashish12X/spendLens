## FAQ — 5 Real Q&As

**Q: Is this actually free?**  
A: Yes. There's no trial period, no credit card, no freemium gotcha. You fill in your tools, get your audit on screen, and can share the link — all free. We ask for an email after you see your results, never before.

**Q: How do you know the right pricing?**  
A: We pull pricing from official vendor pages weekly and cite every source in our PRICING_DATA.md file. Every number in the audit is traceable to a vendor URL. If a price is wrong, file an issue — we'll fix it same day.

**Q: What do you do with my email?**  
A: We send you a copy of your audit. If your savings are significant, a Credex team member may follow up about discounted credits. You can unsubscribe from any email. We don't sell your data.

**Q: Who is Credex and why are they building this?**  
A: Credex sources discounted AI infrastructure credits — Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT Enterprise — from companies that overforecast. We built SpendLens because most startups don't know they're overspending. The audit is genuinely useful on its own, and for high-spend cases, Credex can save you even more.

**Q: What if the audit says I'm already spending correctly?**  
A: We'll tell you. Honestly. "You're spending well" is a real outcome — and we'll explain why each tool is appropriately configured. We don't manufacture savings to manufacture leads. If you're optimal today, we can notify you when new options apply to your stack.
